Who We Are

Parna AI Tech Innovations LLP is a DPIIT-recognised startup, applying artificial intelligence to address critical urban and environmental challenges. Our mission is to bridge the gap between advanced technology and sustainable development.

We specialize in developing applied machine learning solutions that empower cities, governments, and organizations to make data-driven decisions for creating sustainable, resilient, and livable urban environments.

Our work directly contributes to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, focusing on building inclusive, safe, resilient cities (SDG 11) and taking urgent action to combat climate change (SDG 13).
